⚡ Simple Process

How ElderlyConnect works

Three easy steps to get things done or start earning as a student.

01
📝
Post a Task

Elders describe what they need — medicine pickup, floor cleaning, grocery shopping, or anything else. Set a budget and timeline.

02
🎓
Students Apply

Verified student helpers see the task and apply. Elders choose who they're comfortable with based on ratings and profile.

03
✅
Task Done & Paid

Student completes the task, elder confirms completion, payment releases. Both leave reviews for a trusted community.
